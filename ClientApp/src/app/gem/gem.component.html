Who are we?

GeM platform is used by the Ministries/Departments/PSUs/all other Govt organisations to procure goods & Services. Currently there are 34,457 registered buyers on GeM, procuring 873000 products & 6554 Services from GeM platform. Orders worth INR 22,428 Crores have been placed in 1604340 Now of work order.

The policy of Govt. is to promote small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and procure majority of products from SMEs. Currently the Govt. is procuring 41.12% of total procurements from SMEs.

The process of registering company on GeM platform requires extensive documentation and verifications. Further the products/ services which are offered for sale to Govt. has to meet certain quality benchmarks, which includes certifications like ISI, AGMARK, BEE etc. and the products should have a standard design and dimension which is prevalent in market.
